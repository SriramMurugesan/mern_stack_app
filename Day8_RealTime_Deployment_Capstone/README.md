## 👨‍🏫 Instructor Demo Guide (Day 8)

### 1. The "Push vs Pull" Demo (WebSockets)
- **Concept**: Explain the difference between refreshing a page vs. receiving data instantly.
- **Demo**: Open two different browser windows. Send a chat message in one and watch it appear in the other *without a refresh*.
- **Point**: "HTTP is like a post office. WebSockets is like a phone call."

### 2. The "Cloud Photo Album" (Cloudinary)
- **Concept**: Why we don't store photos in our database.
- **Point**: "Databases are for text and relationships. Cloudinary is for media. Keeping them separate makes your app 10x faster and cheaper."

### 3. The "Shipping Container" (Docker)
- **Concept**: "It works on my machine" syndrome.
- **Story**: "Shipping software used to be like shipping loose items on a boat—things break. Docker is like a shipping container. You pack everything your app needs inside, and it runs exactly the same on any computer in the world."
